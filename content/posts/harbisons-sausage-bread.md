## Ingredients
- 1 pkg. bread (3 frozen loaves)
- 1 lbs grated Mozzarella Cheese
- 1 lbs grated Cheddar Cheese
- 1 medium can Parmesan Cheese
- 1 large jar pimento olives (chopped)
- 2 cans pitted black olives
- 1 lbs Owens Sausage (loose hot)
- 1 lbs Owens Smoked Sausage (sliced thin)
- 1 Stick Butter
- 6 Jalapeno Peppers (chopped - no seeds)
- 12 Onion Tops (chopped)

## Directions
Divide loaves and ingredients into six parts. Butter loaves and place in pan to rise. Roll out dough long ways. Rub with butter. Layer as follows: loose sausage, smoked sausage, cheeses, olives, onions/peppers. Fold long side over. Butter again and let sit for 30 mins.

Prep ahead: Bake 350 degrees for 13 minutes. Wrap in foil and freeze.

Bake after prep: Defrost and bake at 350 degrees for 20-25 minutes.

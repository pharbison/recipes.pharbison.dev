## Marinade Ingredients
- 10 chicken tenders
- 1Tbsp salt
- 1Tbsp pepper
- 1Tbsp garlic powder
- 1Tbsp onion powder
- 1Tbsp cayenne pepper
- 1Tbsp chili powder
- 1Tbsp smoked paprika
- 3Tbsp Franks Buffalo Hot Sauce
- 2 cups Buttermilk

## Breading Ingredients
- 2 cups all purpose flour
- 1/2 cup corn starch
- 1Tbsp salt
- 1Tbsp pepper
- 1Tbsp garlic powder
- 1Tbsp onion powder
- 1Tbsp cayenne pepper
- 1Tbsp chili powder
- 1Tbsp smoked paprika

## Hot Honey Sauce Ingredients
3 Tbsp butter
1 tsp garlic powder
1 Tbsp smoked paprika
1/3 cup honey
1/4 cup Frank’s Hot Sauce

## Sauce Directions
Add all ingredients to a pot, simmer for a few minutes on low heat.

## Directions
Marinade chicken for at least 2 hours, overnight if desired. Heat pot of oil to 350F. Toss marinaded chicken in breading and deep fry. Toss fried chicken in sauce or serve sauce seperately.
